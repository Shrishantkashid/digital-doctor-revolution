Diet Recommendation Test

This page tests the diet recommendation feature of the Digital Doctor AI application.

The diet recommendation feature has been successfully integrated into the Digital Doctor AI application. You can now analyze meals by uploading photos and receive nutritional information along with healthier alternatives.

How to use the Diet Analyzer:

  1. Navigate to the Diet Analyzer tool in the main application
  2. Upload a photo of your meal
  3. Select your dietary goals (weight loss, diabetes management, etc.)
  4. Click "Analyze Meal" to get nutritional information
  5. Review the detected items, nutritional content, and healthier alternatives

Features Implemented:

  • Food detection from meal photos
  • Nutritional content estimation
  • Healthier alternative suggestions
  • Regional Indian cuisine support
  • Multi-language support
